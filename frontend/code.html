AgriVision harnesses the power of cutting-edge machine learning to provide farmers with accurate and reliable crop yield predictions. Our sophisticated model analyzes a wide array of data points to deliver actionable insights, helping you make smarter decisions for a more profitable and sustainable farm.

In this project, we have developed an AI-Powered Crop Yield Prediction & Optimization platform that leverages weather data, soil metrics, and historical crop yields to forecast productivity. Along with predictions, our system provides actionable recommendations for irrigation, fertilization, and pest managementтАФhelping farmers increase yields and reduce risks.

How It Works

Our model integrates historical weather patterns, soil health data, satellite imagery, and your specific crop information. Advanced algorithms then process this data to generate highly accurate yield forecasts for your fields.
